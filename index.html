The Roadmap

The short term objective to is very simple

Design and develop a #platform to enable demand driven economy. Meaning, a platform centered around Requestors and Enablers. A pull model, rather than a Push.

Having not for profit as a core value will ensure we don’t compromise on quality on the platform. The platform will aim to source offerings directly from supplier, effectively bringing down cost and enabling quality control through routine inspections by Enablers(Experts)

The long term goal of this platform is to boost local economy by ensure quality products are developed and sourced locally. Sourcing locally will also allow us to enforce accountability and good business practices for the products and services offered through this platform.
